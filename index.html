About Us?

Zarrouk Fruit & Veg is a Moroccan company specializing in the import and export of fresh fruits and vegetables. We aim to be a key link between producers and buyers in global markets. Thanks to our wide network of local and international partners, we ensure the supply of fresh, high-quality agricultural products to markets in Europe, the Middle East, Asia, and North America.

Fresh Vegetables and Fruits Daily

At Zarrouk Fruits and Veg, we are committed to delivering fresh, high-quality fruits and vegetables daily. Our produce is carefully tested for pesticide residues to ensure safe, reliable, and trusted freshness for our valued customers
